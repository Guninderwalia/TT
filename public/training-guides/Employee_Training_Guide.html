TaskTango — Your Quick Guide

Everything you need to know to log time, request leave, and track your own performance. Updated for Production v3.5.

🆕 What's New (V10 → V3.5)

The features that have changed for you since the V10 line. The rest of the guide shows how to use each part of the app.

Chat — A Lot Better Now

The 💬 chat icon moved into the top bar next to your 🔔 notifications. It now does what you'd expect:

  • Pop-up notifications — when a colleague messages you and you're not in the chat panel, your computer shows a desktop notification. Clicking it opens the conversation.
  • Pulsing red badge over the icon when you have unread messages.
  • Auto-open — clicking 💬 jumps straight into your most recent unread thread instead of making you click through the contact list.
  • Emoji picker (😊 button next to the message box) — about 80 popular emojis ready to drop in.
  • File attachments (📎 button) — share screenshots, PDFs, or any file up to 10 MB. Images preview right in the chat; other files open in their default app when clicked.

Mobile / Tablet Friendly

You can now use TaskTango on a phone or tablet — log your time, request leave, check your hours. The layout adjusts automatically: on a phone the sidebar becomes a row of icons across the top.

Profile Pictures

Your profile picture (uploaded by HR or by you on the My Profile page) now shows on your dashboard header avatar and on every chat message you send.

Half-Day Leave

You can now request a half day instead of a full day off — useful for medical appointments or short personal errands.

Leave Balance Rollover

Unused leave at year-end may carry forward or be encashed depending on the policy your HR has configured. Your My Leave Requests page shows the carried-forward balance separately so you can see exactly where your allowance came from.

Getting Started

  1. Open TaskTango — double-click the TaskTango icon on your desktop.
  2. Log in — type your username and password. If this is your first time, the password is password; you'll be asked to change it on first login.
  3. You land on the Dashboard — your home screen, with shortcuts and a quick summary of your account.
  4. Use the menu on the left — every feature lives in its own tab. The next sections explain each one.
Tip: Forgot your password? Ask a department lead or admin to reset it. You'll be given a temporary password and asked to set a new one when you log in.

The Menu, One Tab at a Time

🏠Dashboard

Your home screen. Shows your name, today's date, and a quick at-a-glance summary.

What you do here: Just look around. It's a starting point. If you want to do something specific, click one of the other tabs on the left.

👥Employee Directory

The company phone book. See who works here, in which department, and their email/phone.

What you do here: Look up a colleague's contact details. You can browse by department using the filter at the top.

⏰Time Logging

Where you record what time you started, when you took your lunch break, and when you finished.

What you do here, every working day:

  1. Type or pick today's Start Time when you begin work.
  2. When you go on break, fill in the Break Start time.
  3. When you come back, fill in the Break End time.
  4. At the end of the day, fill in End Time and Save.
Shortcut: If you use the Sign In / Sign Out buttons on the Attendance tab, the system automatically fills your Start and End Times here. You only need to enter your break times.

📅Attendance

Your "I'm here today" button, plus a history of all your past attendance.

What you do here, every working day:

  1. Click Sign In when you arrive — the time is recorded automatically.
  2. Click Sign Out when you're leaving for the day.

The Attendance History table below shows your last 30 days at a glance, including the status (Present / Leave / Absent), sign-in time, sign-out time, and hours worked.

🌴Leave Requests

Request time off and see how many days you have left.

To request leave:

  1. Pick a Start Date and End Date — either by clicking days on the calendar, or by typing the dates directly into the date boxes.
  2. Choose the Leave Type (e.g. Annual Leave).
  3. Write a short Reason (e.g. "family wedding", "doctor appointment").
  4. Click Submit Request. It goes to your department lead for approval.

While waiting: You'll see your request listed as Pending Approval. Once your lead approves, it moves to Pending Final Approval (admin), then becomes Approved.

About your balance: The "Leave Entitlement" box at the top shows how many days you're allowed this year (prorated if you joined partway through the year). The "Leave Balance" panel shows how many you've used and how many remain.

📈My Performance

See how you're scoring on the company's performance metrics.

What you see here: An overall score out of 100, plus a breakdown showing Attendance, Punctuality, Consistency, Lateness, Manager Rating, and Skill ratings. Green is good, yellow is acceptable, red needs attention.

Want a higher score? Show up on time, log your hours every day, keep your time off planned, and ask your manager for skill assessments and ratings.

Frequently Asked Questions

I forgot to sign in this morning. What happens?

Sign in as soon as you remember. The time stamp will reflect when you actually clicked Sign In. If you need it backdated, ask your lead or admin.

Can I request leave for next year already?

Yes, as long as your remaining balance allows it. The Leave Calendar will let you pick future dates.

My leave request is stuck on "Pending Approval" — what now?

That means your department lead hasn't reviewed it yet. Give them a nudge in person or by email if it's urgent.

I see "No leave types available" — why?

Close the Leave Requests page and re-open it once. The system will set up your balance the first time you visit. If it still shows nothing, ask an admin to check that leave types are configured.

The overall score on My Performance is lower than I expected.

Open My Performance and look at the individual columns. Usually the culprit is missing data — for example, you haven't been logging time, or you haven't had any skill assessments yet. Talk to your manager.

How do I change my password?

Click the Password button near your name in the bottom-left corner of the screen.
